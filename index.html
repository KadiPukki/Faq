Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve seats with miles?

You can use miles to buy a seat for an existing flight reservation. To do this, you will find your trip and log in to your AAdvantage account, if you have not already, check that you chose AAdvantage Miles as the payment on Review and pay.

How much do seats cost if I use miles?

Seat pricing depends on several factors including flight length and seat location. You can check seat prices when you view your trip details.

Who is eligible to reserve seats with miles?

AAdvantage members traveling in the Main Cabin on flights marketed and operated by American can use AAdvantage miles to reserve a seat after booking1 exclusively on the My Trip page. AAdvantage members may use miles to reserve seats for themselves and for other travelers.

Can I change my seat though I have already paid for it?

Yes, you can choose a seat of equal or lesser value at no extra charge. Keep in mind refunds are not issued if you choose a seat of lesser value. If you wpuld like to buy a seat at a greater value, we will refund your original purchase afterward. Please allow up to 48 hours for processing.
